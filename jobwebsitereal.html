Student job site's vision is to make the students Self Employed

To create a welcoming environment respectful of individual unique backgrounds, beliefs, and goals. To exceed the expectations of our internal and external constituents through open communication and responsiveness.

To promote student responsibility for lifelong career development through exploration, education, and experience. To develop and strengthen relationships with employers to create opportunities for students. To partner with stakeholders across the University to enhance and support student achievement.

As a result of participating in the services or utilizing resources offered by the Student Jobsitet, students will be able to: Formulate career goals, including choice of major, based on self-knowledge of interests, values, personality, and skills .
